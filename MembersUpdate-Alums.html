Alumni Members:

Welcome to all of our Tate Society Alumni!

We'd love to stay in touch, hear what amazing things you're up to, and invite you to reunion events. With only a scribbled signature in the Member Book, however, it's too easy for future generations of Taters to lose touch. Feel free to contact us and let us know how you're doing!

With thanks and forever Tate Love,
The Tate Society Executive Board
