Important Updates and Reminders

This page should be updated regularly. If you can't find what you're looking for, contact us, and check back later for more information.

The next upcoming Tate event is:

February 3rd in Coverdell

Other upcoming events include:

As we are moving into full-fledged planning for the spring semester recruiting, we wanted to put some things on your radar that we'll be asking for shortly:

First, please think of some stellar first-years you'd love to see apply and text/email/call them! We'd love to have as many applications as possible.

Second, Because we get a couple hundred applications, each with 2 essays, we rely on y'all to help us grade them. The more graders we have, the more objective and equitable we can make the process (by averaging out subjectivity unique to individual graders). This is a really fun way (we promise, just ask the people who did it last year) to be involved in the selection process. It's also generally helpful to be on this side of a grading process (ask Chip about the 3 things he's learned to never do in an essay because of grading Tate essays). If you'd be willing to read a few dozen essays some time around the 2nd week of February, sign up using this link.

Last, unrelated to our push for applications, we're looking for nominations from you guys for honorary members of the Tate Society for this year. You can find all of our previous honorary members on the members tab of the website, just for reference. Please nominate honorary members here.

Things to know:

Applications for the 2019 class of tatertots will be made available on January 16th and will be due on February 8th.

Also, as a general reminder, all of the second-years (per the new requirements enacted last year) are required to attend one Tate event each year.
